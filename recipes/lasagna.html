Lasagna Recipe

Description :

Lasagna is a dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, typically cooked in an oven and layered with a sauce, cheese, and sometimes meat or vegetables

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Canned tomatoes

Steps :

  1. Cook the Meat
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
